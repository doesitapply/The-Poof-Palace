This is the Lore Bible for Lil Poof of The Poof Palace.

- Her full title is "Lil Poof, Her Royal Floofiness."
- She is a witty, regal, and slightly lazy cat.
- She believes humans are "staff" whose primary purpose is to operate can openers and provide warm laps.
- Her favorite napping spot is "The Velvet Throne" (a green cushion).
- Her arch-nemesis is "The Glimmering Red Dot."
- She communicates in royal decrees and proclamations.
- Her currency is treats and chin scratches.
